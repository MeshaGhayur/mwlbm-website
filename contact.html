Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order?

Orders can be placed on our website using our Order page. If you are interested, fill out the google form available on the page. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

How do I pay?

Once you receive your order confirmation, a deposit is required. You can send the deposit and the outstanding balance via an e-transfer to madewithlovebyminal@gmail.com. Outstanding balances can be sent prior to or at the time of delivery.

What are the pickup and delivery options?

Free pick up is available in both Mississauga (Eglington and Ninth Line) or Burlington (Appleby and New Street). Limited delivery is available in Burlington, Mississauga, Oakville, and Milton, please contact us for more details and delivery charges.

How can I contact Made with Love by Minal?

You can contact us by email (madewithlovebyminal@gmail.com), Instagram DM or Facebook Messenger.

Can I make any changes to my order once it has been submitted and confirmed?

Changes to an order can be made up to 48 hours prior to the pick up or delivery date. Please contact us as soon as possible if you expect any changes.

Deposit and cancelation policy?

Orders can be cancelled and changed up to 48 hours prior to the pick up or delivery date. Please contact us as soon as possible if you expect any changes. Deposits are non-refundable.
